Job Title: Data Analyst

Role Overview:
We are seeking a highly motivated and detail-oriented Data Analyst who can transform raw data
into meaningful insights that drive business decisions. The ideal candidate will have strong
analytical skills, business understanding, and the ability to communicate insights clearly to
stakeholders.

Key Responsibilities:
• Collect, clean, and validate large datasets from multiple sources
• Analyze data to identify trends, patterns, and actionable insights
• Create dashboards and reports using tools like Power BI / Tableau
• Write and optimize SQL queries for data extraction and analysis
• Work closely with business teams to understand requirements
• Perform data validation and ensure data accuracy
• Present insights and recommendations to stakeholders
• Support decision-making through data-driven analysis

Required Skills:
• Strong knowledge of SQL and databases
• Proficiency in Excel (VLOOKUP, Pivot Tables, Power Query)
• Experience with Power BI / Tableau
• Basic to intermediate Python (Pandas, NumPy)
• Understanding of statistics (mean, median, variance, confidence interval)
• Strong problem-solving and analytical thinking

Preferred Qualifications:
• Bachelor’s degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or related field
• Experience working with large datasets
• Knowledge of data modeling and ETL concepts
• Familiarity with business metrics and KPIs

Why Join Us:
• Opportunity to work with real-world data and impactful projects
• Collaborative and growth-focused work environment
• Learning and career development opportunities
• Competitive salary and benefits

Location: Open / Hybrid / Remote
Experience: 0–3 Years
